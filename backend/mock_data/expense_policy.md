# Corporate T&E and Expense Policy
_Last revised: Q1 2026 — Finance & Compliance Office_

## Section 4.2 — Client & Team Meals
Meals with clients or internal team meetings are reimbursable up to **$150 per person**. Any
meal exceeding this cap requires written pre-approval from a Vice President or above. Alcohol
ordered as part of a client meal is reimbursable only if it does not exceed 20% of the total bill.

## Section 6.1 — Lodging
Hotel and lodging costs are capped at **$400 per night** in Tier-1 cities (New York, San
Francisco, London, Tokyo) and **$250 per night** in all other locations. Bookings above the cap
must be justified with a business reason and approved by the traveler's manager before booking.

## Section 8.3 — Software & SaaS
Individual software or SaaS purchases up to $5,000 may be expensed directly by a department
head. Purchases **over $5,000** require sign-off from the CTO's office and must be logged in the
Software Asset Registry prior to purchase.

## Section 9.5 — Prohibited Categories
The following categories are **never reimbursable**, regardless of amount: nightclubs, bars or
lounges when not tied to a documented client entertainment plan, spa services, gambling or
casino expenses, and any personal grooming expenses. Employees who submit these will have the
full amount rejected and may be referred to HR for repeated violations.

## Section 12.0 — General Business Expense
Any business expense not covered by a more specific category above is capped at **$1,000** and
requires standard manager approval in the expense system prior to reimbursement.
